    model = m
    model.fit(X_train, y_train)
    y_pred = model.predict(X_test)
    print('Accuracy:{:.2f} Precision:{:.2f} Recall:{:.2f} '.format(accuracy_score(y_test, y_pred),

Conclusion¶

On the complete data, Logistic Regression proved to have higher precision and recall, but the same accuracy, so in general this model gives us better results. If we start to evaluate different features to find redundant, we discover that removing the Contract Period feature does improve metrics in both models - precision and recall for Random Forest Classifier, and accuracy for Logistic Regression. Removing other features had no positive impact on model metrics. So the best option is to train the Logistic Regression model on a dataset with the Contract Period feature dropped.

Step 5. Overall conclusions and basic recommendations on working with customers¶

The research was conducted to shed a light on features that influence customer's decision to quit working out in our gym chain. Based on machine learning models, we may name the main features:

  1. Proximity to a gym. There's an entire cohort of users identified by the ML model, wich live far from any gym of our chain, but still prefer us. Churn rate is high in this group, so we should consider opening more locations close to those customers.
  2. Social features, which include group workouts, bring a friend promo, partner's employees: all this decrease churn rate for the participants. It might be good to develop more group activities and programs.
  3. Time-related features: contract period, lifetime, average visits for last month. The most churn happens during the first month in a gym. We should come up with ideas on how to help users to overcome the first workout month. Also, we should pay close attention to customers who lower their average weekly visits, as it is a sign they might leave soon.
  4. Age and additional spendings: there's a cohort of younger customers who spend much less on additional services and have a high churn rate and also lower weekly visits. We might assume they are students with lower income and less time. A student-oriented program might help lower the churn rate beneath them.
