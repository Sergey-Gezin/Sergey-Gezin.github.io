Conclusion:¶

Since RICE takes into account the Reach parameter, the prioritization changed. Hypotheses with similar Effort but smaller Reach will have lower priority. Considering Reach automatically drops the birthday hypothesis from the first position on ICE to fifth on RICE - the Reach is very low, hence the outcome in revenue will be low and may be the company should focus on those hypotheses which may improve revenue as much as possible, with reasonable effort. Nevertheless, this drop was the biggest of them all, most of the changes are not that big, and RICE just looks more precise in prioritization, taking into account more factors. So using RICE is justified when company needs the changes to affect maximum amount of customers.
