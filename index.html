About Me.

I had a job as a project manager in a small survey company. But then the corona hit, and like millions of other people, I was grounded in the four walls of my home.


The only thing that was left for me to do, is to monitor the world situation and in Israel, where I live. I was fascinated, how all the corona statistic websites look. I learned that it is called a dashboard. They all were interactive, beautiful, and most important, very informative. I thought you need to be a programming genius to build something like that. But luckily, I was wrong. Soon I've stumbled upon a Yandex Data Analyst course in Israel - they were recruiting 100 participants. I went through all the screening processes and have been selected to the first cohort from more than 3000 candidates.


In the following six months I've learned to work with data professionally, to do a complete data analysis using Python and SQL, to analyze all the business metrics, conduct A/B tests, building dashboards with BI tools, and many other things.


So now I'm looking for the oportunities to start my new career as Data Analyst. Please check my portfolio, and I'll be glad for any feedback.
